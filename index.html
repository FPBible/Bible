Fulfilled Bible

New Creation Paraphrase

This project presents Scripture through the lens of Christ’s completed work and the fully established New Covenant. Prophetic language is read as Scripture itself uses it — covenantally, symbolically, and with audience relevance — not as prediction of a still-future end of the world.

The Fulfilled Bible is a modern paraphrase and commentary project that reads the biblical story as reaching its climax in Christ, with the Old Covenant brought to its conclusion and the New Creation now fully present.
